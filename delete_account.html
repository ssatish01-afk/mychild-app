Delete Account & Data

You can delete your MyChild account and all associated data at any time. This page explains how to request account deletion and what data will be removed.

How to delete your account
1
Open the MyChild app on your Android device
2
Tap the sign out icon on the home screen (top right)
3
To delete your account completely, email us at support@mychild.app with subject "Delete My Account"
4
We will delete your account and all data within 30 days of your request
⚠️ This action is permanent. Once deleted, your account and all your children's health records cannot be recovered.
Data that will be deleted
✓ Your account and login information
✓ All children's profiles and health records
✓ Vaccine records and immunisation history
✓ Growth measurements and charts
✓ Lab reports and medical history
✓ Doctor visit records
✓ Meal plans and nutrition data
✓ Uploaded photos and documents
✓ All notifications and preferences
Data retention period

All personal data is permanently deleted within 30 days of receiving your deletion request. No data is retained after this period.

Contact us for account deletion:

📧 Email: support@mychild.app
Subject: Delete My Account

Please include your registered phone number in the email so we can identify your account.
