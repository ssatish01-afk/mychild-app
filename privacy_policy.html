1. Introduction

Welcome to MyChild — Your Child's Complete Health Tracker. MyChild ("the App") is developed and operated by Satish Sundaram ("I", "me", or "my"). I am committed to protecting the privacy and security of your personal information and your children's health data.

This Privacy Policy explains how I collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use the MyChild mobile application. By using the App, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy.

2. Information I Collect

2.1 Account Information

  • Mobile phone number (used for OTP-based login)
  • Name and profile information
  • Google account details (if you sign in with Google)

2.2 Children's Health Information

I collect the following information about your children, which you voluntarily provide:

  • Full name, date of birth, sex, and blood group
  • Vaccine records and immunisation history
  • Growth measurements (height, weight, head circumference)
  • Lab reports and medical analysis results
  • Medical history and doctor visit records
  • Meal plans and nutrition information
  • Profile photos

2.3 Usage Information

  • App usage patterns and features accessed
  • Device information (device type, operating system version)
  • Crash reports and performance data

3. How I Use Your Information

I use the information collected to:

  • Create and manage your account
  • Store and display your children's health records
  • Generate AI-powered analysis of lab reports using Anthropic's Claude API
  • Send vaccine and appointment reminder notifications
  • Generate health passport QR codes
  • Improve the App's features and performance
  • Provide customer support
  • Comply with applicable laws and regulations

4. AI Analysis and Third-Party Services

4.1 Anthropic Claude AI

When you upload lab reports for AI analysis, the report content is sent to Anthropic's Claude API for processing. This data is processed solely to generate the health analysis and is not stored or used by Anthropic for training purposes.

4.2 Firebase (Google)

I use Google Firebase for authentication, cloud database storage (Firestore), and file storage (Firebase Storage). Firebase's data processing is governed by Google's Privacy Policy.

4.3 Google Play Billing

Subscription payments are processed by Google Play. I do not store your payment card information.

5. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored on Google Firebase servers located in the asia-south1 (Mumbai, India) region. Security measures include:

  • All data transmission encrypted using TLS/HTTPS
  • Firestore security rules ensure users can only access their own data
  • Firebase Storage rules restrict file access to account owner only
  • Authentication required for all data access

6. Children's Privacy

MyChild is designed for parents and guardians to manage children's health records. The App is not intended for direct use by children. All accounts must be created and managed by adults (18 years and older).

7. Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023 (DPDP Act)

As an Indian app collecting personal data, I comply with India's Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023. Your rights under this Act include:

  • Right to access your personal data
  • Right to correction of inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Right to erasure of your personal data
  • Right to grievance redressal
  • Right to nominate a person to exercise rights in the event of your death or incapacity

8. Data Retention

Your data is retained for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, your personal data and children's health records will be permanently deleted within 30 days.

9. Data Sharing

I do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. Information may be shared only with service providers (Firebase, Anthropic) strictly for providing the App's services, or when required by law.

10. Your Rights and Choices

  • Account Deletion: Delete your account at any time from within the App
  • Notification Preferences: Manage reminders through your device's notification settings
  • Data Export: Export your child's health passport as a PDF at any time

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. You will be notified of material changes through a prominent notice in the App or via SMS.
